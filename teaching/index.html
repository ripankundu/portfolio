Teaching Philosophy

My teaching approach emphasizes hands-on learning, critical thinking, and practical application of theoretical concepts. Through my experience mentoring undergraduate researchers, I focus on:

  • Guiding students through the complete research process - from problem identification to publication
  • Fostering independent thinking and problem-solving skills
  • Encouraging collaboration and peer learning
  • Providing individualized feedback and support
  • Bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world applications
